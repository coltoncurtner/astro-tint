Office tint FAQ

Good questions, straight answers.

What can office window film do?

Office film cuts solar heat and screen glare, adds daytime privacy, and can frost or brand conference-room glass. It also lowers cooling costs and protects interiors from UV fading.

Can you add frosted or branded privacy glass?

Yes. We install frosted and decorative films for conference rooms, glass partitions and entry doors, and can cut custom logos or patterns for branded privacy.

Will office tint make it too dark inside?

No. Modern solar-control films reject heat and glare while staying clear, so you keep natural light and the view. We'll match the film to your light and privacy goals.

Do you work around office hours?

Yes — we schedule around your team, including evenings and weekends, so the install doesn't interrupt work.
