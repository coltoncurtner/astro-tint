Home tint FAQ

Good questions, straight answers.

What does home window tinting do?

Residential film cuts heat and glare, evens out hot and cold rooms, adds daytime privacy, blocks over 99% of UV to protect floors and furniture from fading, and can lower cooling costs in sunny rooms.

Will home tint make my rooms dark?

No. We offer light, nearly invisible films that reject heat and UV while keeping your natural light and view. We match the film to each room's exposure and your goals.

Can you give me daytime privacy without losing the view?

Yes. Reflective and dual-reflective films provide strong daytime privacy from the outside while you still see out. For 24/7 privacy we can use frosted or decorative films.

Does window film really protect furniture from fading?

Yes. Our films block over 99% of UV, the main cause of fading, which helps protect hardwood floors, rugs, artwork and furniture.
